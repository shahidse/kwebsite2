Instructions for Resonance Healing App

Resonance Healing.ai is designed to be user-friendly, making any kind of remedy easy. Just call up the Potentiser screen, type in the name of the remedy required, the Rate, or simply the word "COPY," select the potency you require (X, C, D, M, CM, MM, LM), and power level information for amplified remedies. Set the timer period for 3 - 30 seconds, place your blank tablets, glass of water, or other medium in the Receive well, press ENTER, and your remedy is ready.

If you wish, you can then "program" that remedy with specific qualities, actually shaping your remedy to fit your requirement, and then lock it into the carrier medium so that it will never fade or lose potency, even if touched by the hand or placed next to strong-smelling substances.

Alternatively, if you wish to make a multiple remedy with various ingredients and potencies, this can be done in one operation using the Potentiser screen. Simply enter the remedy information into the four data fields. You can create and mix Flower, Gem, and Homoeopathic Remedies, both with and without Homoeopathic Potencies, in the time it takes to type the information in, plus a few seconds of potentising time.

A record of the remedies you have used is automatically created. You can also make permanently saved copies of your remedy combinations and easily recall them later.

The keyword here is flexibility. If you prefer to use rates, no problem. Just type your rate in, and you can even enter frequencies and waveforms to discover a whole new world of therapeutic possibilities.
